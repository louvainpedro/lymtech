Use BPMN to easily visualize orchestration flows

  • The Process Modeling and Notation Standard 2.0 (BPMN 2.0) provides a way to create a graphical diagram of business processes and convert them into automated processes.
  • Use it to design a process visually.
  • The graphical process modeling tool allows you to add human and automatic tasks, gateways with decision rules to join and split the process flow, subprocesses and much more.
  • BPMN makes it easier to understand end-to-end logic and collaborate during orchestration flow definition.

Microservices and distributed systems

  • Bonita can be used to extract business logic from distributed processes or services and to provide visibility into call sequences.
  • Advantages of using Bonita for orchestration:
  • Automatic error handling when they occur, using exception routes, timers and other BPMN elements. If human intervention is necessary, it can also be included in the logic.
  • Bonita's BPMN engine stores all workflow executions in a database so they can be analyzed later.
  • Data on cases and processes can be collected for reporting.
  • The BPMN allows you to define data distribution and processing rules, to ensure that tasks are assigned to the appropriate services and people.

Connect to RPA tools and manage RPA robots

  • Use Bonita with RPA solutions to automate processes end-to-end, with exception management, complex decision making by humans, and delegation of repetitive task sequences to software robots.
  • RPA automates repetitive, rule-based tasks, typically performed by humans, to manipulate data and applications as a human would.
  • Integrate RPA robots as part of your comprehensive processes at Bonita.

Delegate repetitive task sequences to UiPath robots

  • Call UiPath robots from Bonita business processes and applications using connectors and user interfaces.
  • Automates integration with existing systems that cannot be accessed via API.
  • Connect to the UiPath Orchestrator server to interact with robots on your network.
