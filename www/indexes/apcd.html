About the Client

APCD (Paulista Association of Dentist Surgeons), with over 100 years in the market, is a non-profit organization that holds a prominent place in the world of dentistry in Brazil. Represented throughout the state of São Paulo through its 88 Regional Offices, it has over 40,000 members. In 2020, APCD faced the challenge of hosting its annual congress virtually due to the global pandemic. To achieve this goal, APCD relied on Lymtech to develop a technological solution that would allow the event to take place 100% online.

The Challenge

The main challenge was to transform an in-person event into a robust and functional digital platform, ensuring the participation and interaction of thousands of attendees. This involved:

Integrating multiple technologies.

Ensuring an intuitive and seamless user experience.

Guaranteeing the stability and security of the platform

Meeting an extremely short development deadline.

The Solution

Lymtech helped in the development of a web an mobile application so that APCD could hold the congress online. This implied the need to involve severall technologies such as Azure Cloud, Python, SAP and Vimeo as Live Streaming.

The Result

The event could be done in Pearson, but had more than 2000 online attendees who were able to enjoy and interact on the congress. In addition, a backoffice was developed with a bootchar, organization of stands, agenda, registration to the talks, key notes, among others.
