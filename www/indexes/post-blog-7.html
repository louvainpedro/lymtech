Integration with Marketplaces


In the digitalized world we live in, marketplaces have become a fundamental part of the success of businesses of all sizes, as we can see from the McKinsey & Company study, “companies that work on marketplaces have 2.5 times revenue growth bigger than companies that don’t work on marketplaces.” These online sales channels offer a wide variety of benefits for retailers, such as:

Access to a larger audience: Marketplaces like Amazon, Mercado Livre and Shopee attract millions of buyers around the world. This means that retailers can reach a much larger audience than they could on their own.

Ease of operation: offer a ready-to-use platform, which reduces the need for investment in technology or infrastructure. And combined with our process management solution, you can have these platforms integrated into your systems.

Cost reduction: Marketplaces tend to charge lower fees than their own online stores.




Integration with marketplaces offers a series of benefits for companies, including:



Increased sales: Marketplaces offer a large potential audience for retailers. This can lead to a significant increase in sales. And this visualization becomes even greater with the support of our solution.

Expanding into new markets: Marketplaces can help companies expand into new geographic markets.

Improved brand visibility: Marketplaces can help companies increase their brand visibility.




To integrate with marketplaces, companies need to follow a few steps:


Choose the right marketplaces: Not all marketplaces are created equal. Some platforms are better suited for certain types of products or services. Therefore, it is important to research the available options and choose those that are most relevant to your business.

Prepare your products: Before starting to sell on marketplaces, it is important to prepare your products for online sale. This includes creating high-quality photos and descriptions, setting competitive prices, and setting up inventory.

Set up your account: Each marketplace has a different setup process. Therefore, it is important to follow the marketplace's instructions to configure your account correctly.

Start selling: After setting up your account, you're ready to start selling on marketplaces.



Tips for a successful integration!


Define your objectives: Before starting to integrate your business with marketplaces, it is important to define your objectives. Do you want to increase sales? Expand into new markets? Or improve your brand’s visibility?

Provide good customer service: Customer service is critical to success in any sales channel. Therefore, it is important to offer quality service to your customers on marketplaces.

Use technology to your advantage: By hiring Lymtech's service, your entire sales process can be automated. Thus reducing the time needed to execute your sales, your costs and also helping to improve decision making.


Conclusion



A fluid and fast integration with marketplaces is essential to maximize online sales opportunities and improve operational efficiency. Real-time synchronization of information and automation of processes provides a more positive purchasing experience for customers and increases the competitiveness of companies in the digital market.
