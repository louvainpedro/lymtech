Process Automation

Process automation

Currently, many companies are investing in the automation of business processes with the objective of increasing its revenues, operational efficiencies and delivering a exceptional experience for your customers. But after all, what is automation? of business processes?

A automating business processes is a way that companies have found to improve its organizational efficiency, through the use of digital technologies to run recurring processes. One of the premises of this execution is the almost no human intervention in these processes.

Ao delegate the execution of routine tasks to machines, employees who previously dedicated their time to these functions can be reallocated to tasks that add more value to the company.

Check out the benefits of automation of processes for your company:

  • Standardization process execution


    In a company, the same process can be carried out by different employees for a variety of reasons. In this sense, the automation of processes can put an end to this problem as it will now be always executed in the same way by a machine, increasing the consistency of results.


  • History auditable


    Automation solutions have each step of your process saved in a traceable history. In addition to being able to see the entire process history, you can also see who made any changes and when. This benefit significantly increases the transparency and security of company data.


  • Error minimization


    The machines are soon configured and loaded with the necessary rules to perform their tasks with incredible precision. Even the most capable employee is susceptible to making mistakes when performing recurring and repetitive tasks.


  • Increased efficiency


    Process automation has as one of its characteristics the execution of tasks using as few resources as possible. This factor implies directly on the company's efficiency.


  • Economy of time


    By transferring responsibility for performing recurring tasks in the company to machines, employees who previously performed them can dedicate their time to other functions that add more value to the company as a whole.


  • Increased productivity


    By using technology as an ally to assist in some tasks, company employees can dedicate their time to other needs, generating more productivity for the company in general.


  • Improvement of compliance


    Automation solutions simplify regulatory compliance. This is due to the implementation of security controls, such as data encryption.


  • Improvement in the consumer experience


    With automated processes, employees have fewer worries and more free time to dedicate themselves to offering excellent service to their customers. Other direct benefits to the consumer experience are faster response, data-driven customizations, and greater consistency between several different channels.


  • Scalability


    Machine-automated processes have fantastic scalability when compared to those done manually. This is extremely important for companies in full growth and that have great volatility in their processes. A clear example of this would be a significant increase in demand from one month to the next. A human would take longer to adapt to this increase, whereas a machine can be loaded with new rules and data and that's it: it's ready to meet the company's needs.
